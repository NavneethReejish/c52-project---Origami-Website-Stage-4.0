Chameleons

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important mode of communication among chameleons.
  • Unlike many lizards, Chameleons can't regrow their tails.

Camels

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels have humps, that allow them to store up to 80 pounds of fat, which they can live off for weeks, or even months!
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them eat thorny plants like succulents, which other animals can't eat.

Bears

Interesting Facts about Bears

  • Bears are one of the most intelligent land animals in North America.
  • Bears care deeply about family members.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.

Pandas

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • They can camouflage in their natural habitat.
  • They spend most of their day eating.
  • They are found mostly in thick bamboo forests, that they call home.
